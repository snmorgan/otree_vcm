This is a VCM game with 5 players.

An individual from the group will be randomly selected to be the decision-maker. All other participants will receive an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }}; the decision-maker receives nothing. Individuals will be asked to submit comments about a minimum contribution rule to the group account. The decision-maker will read the comments and will choose a minimum contribution rule. Participants will be provided with the chosen rule, and will then choose how many tokens to allocate to the group account. Individuals will repeat this process for 5 rounds under the chosen rule.
